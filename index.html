Install on your phone (recommended)

Installing pins it to your home screen and stores everything locally so it works without cell signal.

  1. Open this page in Safari (it must be Safari on iOS, not Chrome).
  2. Tap the Share button (square with an up-arrow) at the bottom.
  3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Tap Add in the upper right.
  5. On your home screen, find the new SRS Trip icon (shown below) and tap it to launch the app.
Don't skip this -- open SRS Trip once more on cell signal. After it's on your home screen, launch the SRS Trip app and let it sit for a few seconds while still on cell signal. That's when the daily itinerary, full reference, GPX, icons, and offline base map all get cached. After that it works fully offline in the Swell.

Offline tip

Open the app once while on cell signal so the service worker can cache the daily itinerary, the reference doc, the GPX file, the icons, and the offline base map. After that the app works fully offline. When you next reach signal, the app silently checks for updates and shows a "Tap to reload" prompt if a new version is available.
