Alternate itinerary A — Forward, 4-day Swell lighten (A-V2)

Not the primary GPX. This is the Option A day-by-day plan: same Wedge-first direction as the main trip, with Family Butte on May 4, Behind-the-Reef without tactical slot hikes on May 5, and slot + Sinbad work on May 6 for stay-overs. Early-leavers peel off May 6 after a shared send-off (~09:00–09:15) toward Boise.

Variant: A-V2 — do not run to Moab the night of May 6; May 7 highway to Moab / Ken’s Lake + easy afternoon (e.g. Fins N Things, Sand Flats — confirm permits/fees).

Overview: overland-alternates.html

Day 0 — Fri May 2

Same as main plan: travel to Black Dragon-area staging / camp (see primary itinerary).

Day 1 — Sat May 3 (Swell)

Unchanged vs main: Wedge corridor and camp at Wedge Overlook / Little Grand Canyon.

Day 2 — Sun May 4 (Swell) — shortened

Stops (order): Wedge → River Crossing → The Drips → Eva Conover Trail → Eagle Canyon cluster (Overlook / Bridges / Trail / Arch) → Icebox → Swasey’s Cabin → Loan Warrior Petroglyph → camp Family Butte Dispersed (38.76868, -110.83217; day2_swell tertiary in data).

Notes: ~50 route mi vs ~72 on main. Reds Canyon, Lucky Strike, Tomsich, Hondu move to May 5. Family Butte is first-come; tertiary note in data ~6–8 vehicles — group may spill to Reds Canyon Rd dispersed.

Day 3 — Mon May 5 (Swell) — shortened driving, no slot hikes en route

Stops: Family Butte → Reds Canyon → Lucky Strike Mine → Tomsich Butte uranium / mine POI → Hondu Arch → Hidden Splendor Overlook → Miner’s Cabin → Behind-the-Reef (drive full traverse; do not schedule Chute / Crack / LWH hikes midday — drive past) → Temple Mountain Townsite camp (primary day3_swell).

Optional evening: carpool Wild Horse Window from Temple Mtn (~1.5 hr RT) for golden hour.

Target: camp Temple Mtn ~16:00–17:00.

Day 4 — Tue May 6

Whole group (morning)

  • ~07:30 breakfast at Temple Mtn.
  • Shared first stop (pick one): Temple Wash Petroglyphs / North Temple Wash narrows if WHW was done May 5 evening; otherwise Wild Horse Window carpool (~1.5 hr).
  • ~09:00–09:15 split (Temple Mtn or Hwy 24): early-leavers → Hwy 24 N → I-70 W → Boise (on pavement by 09:30 target).

Stay-overs (rest of May 6)

  • West on Behind-the-Reef to Chute and/or Crack trailheads (tactical; flood check — see slot-canyon-guide.html).
  • Return east → Tunnel → Head of Sinbad → Dutchman Arch → I-70 E toward Moab — stop short for night: Goblin Valley SP, Temple Mtn again, or Hwy 24 dispersed (not Ken’s Lake same night).

Day 5 — Wed May 7 (travel / Moab)

  • Morning: I-70 E to Moab / Ken’s Lake.
  • Afternoon: recovery trail — planned Fins N Things (BLM Sand Flats); confirm group appetite and fees.

After May 7

Continue with the main Moab segment as already planned for the trip (this alt only adjusts Swell exit timing and compresses Moab by one calendar day vs the baseline if Moab was counted from original Day 5 arrival — align with your master calendar).
