## Why split at Wedge (not Swinging Bridge)

Swinging Bridge sits far **N** in Buckhorn; reaching it before split burns **hours**; Castle Dale exit still requires **south** hookup to Green River Cutoff — **~20 mi backtrack** for early-leavers. **Wedge** puts the **N/NW** exit immediately.

---

## Trade-offs (short)

- **Pro:** Softer than **B** Day 1; **HS Overlook** on **May 4**; **Family Butte** + **Wedge** use existing catalog camps.
- **Con:** Early-leavers **miss** full Buckhorn / Black Dragon string (stay-overs get it May 6).
- **Con:** May 3 camp is **dispersed / first-come** at new pins — scout or fallback to **Miner’s Cabin** area if full.
