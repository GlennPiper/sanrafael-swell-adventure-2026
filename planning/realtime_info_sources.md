## Cell Coverage

Cell coverage in the Swell is **VERY limited**. Expect:
- No coverage in: Black Dragon Canyon interior, Buckhorn Draw (spotty), Reds Canyon, Behind the Reef, most canyon bottoms
- Partial (Verizon / AT&T): top of Buckhorn Draw, Wedge Overlook (sometimes), Temple Mountain area
- Full coverage: Green River, Moab, major I-70 / Hwy 191 corridors

**Plan to download all real-time data the morning of each day** while in Green River (Day 2 morning), or while at I-70 rest stops. For each day after Day 1, offline reference (in `trip-reference.html`) plus a quick refresh at any town stop is the strategy.

## Pre-Trip Checklist (run morning of May 1 and May 2)

1. NWS forecast for each bookmark above (check for rain, wind, cold)
2. Flash-flood watches/warnings for Emery County, Wayne County
3. UDOT I-70 and Hwy 191 for construction/closures Boise -> Swell -> Moab
4. Utah Fire Info -- any active fires in central/SE UT? Any Stage 1/2 fire restrictions (no campfires)?
5. BLM Price + Moab offices news pages for trail/road closures
6. AirNow Fire & Smoke -- any significant plumes forecast to drift over the route?
7. Verify Arches NP timed-entry status for May 6-9
8. Stream gauge on San Rafael River -- flow rate reasonable (<200 cfs typically means easy crossings)

## Morning-of checklist (each day on trail)

Only possible at fuel/town stops. Order of priority when connected:
1. Active flash-flood watches for Emery / Wayne / Grand county
2. NWS 12-hr hourly for current location
3. UDOT if any interstate portion that day
4. Group InReach check-in to home base
