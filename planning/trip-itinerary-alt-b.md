# Alternate itinerary B — Reverse (Sinbad-first), Variant V1

**Not the primary GPX.** **Option B** runs the Swell **west → east** (Temple / Sinbad side first, Buckhorn / Black Dragon last). This page uses **Variant V1** by default: stay-overs reach **Moab / Sand Flats the evening of May 6** after any catch-up miles. (**V2** = extra Swell night May 6, Moab May 7 — optional.)

**Overview:** [overland-alternates.html](../overland-alternates.html)

## May 1 — Fri (meet + Bonneville)

Same as **main** May 1 meet + Bonneville overnight.

---

## May 2 — Sat (staging: Temple Mtn side)

**Staging:** Bonneville corridor → Green River → **S on Hwy 24** → **Temple Mountain Townsite** (FCFS) or Goblin Valley dispersed. **Not** Black Dragon staging.

---

## Day 1 — Sat May 3 (Swell) — heavy day

Temple Mtn → **Dutchman Arch** → **Head of Sinbad** → **Tunnel** → **North Temple Wash** → **Wild Horse Window** → **1–2 slot hikes** (Chute / Crack per group) → **Behind-the-Reef** (full traverse) → **camp Tomsich Butte** (dispersed / mine area per data).

**Note:** Longest driving+hike day of the trip; matches roughly **main Day 3 + Day 4 AM** compressed while everyone is fresh.

---

## Day 2 — Sun May 4 (Swell)

Tomsich → **Hondu** / mine POIs → **Reds Canyon** → **Lucky Strike** → **Loan Warrior** → Swasey’s → Icebox → **Eagle Canyon** cluster → **Eva Conover** → Drips → Coal Wash → Fuller Bottom → **camp Wedge Overlook** / Little Grand Canyon.

---

## Day 3 — Mon May 5 (Swell)

Wedge → Dinosaur Footprint → Buckhorn Petroglyphs → Buckhorn Wash → Split Rock → Red Canyon → Swinging Bridge → Sinkhole → Black Dragon Canyon → Black Dragon Petroglyph.

**Camp (pick one):**

- **Black Dragon** dispersed near **I-70 Exit 147** — early-leavers: minimal trail May 6; easy **09:30** pavement.
- **Wedge** (nicer camp) — early-leavers: shared **Wedge sunrise**, then **Buckhorn Rd** north → **E Green River Cutoff** / **Maverik Castle Dale** (~50–70 min graded/paved) → **Hwy 10** → home routing.

---

## Day 4 — Tue May 6

- **Early-leavers:** per **May 5** camp choice above; on pavement by **09:30** target.
- **Stay-overs (V1):** anything **skipped Day 1** (extra slot, BTR segment if abbreviated), then **I-70 E** to **Moab / Sand Flats** — plan **same evening** as main Moab arrival (adjust exact hour to field reality).

---

## Moab and after

Align with the **main** trip’s Moab block; **V1** assumes **no** extra Swell night **May 6**.

## Trade-offs (short)

- **Pro:** Early-leavers can stage off **true highway** (Black Dragon) or **Wedge + Castle Dale** exit.
- **Pro:** Rested drivers for **Behind-the-Reef** early in the trip.
- **Con:** Loses “save the best for last” vs forward narrative; **fuel / resupply story** reads backward — see [fuel-plan.html](../fuel-plan.html) (still written for **forward** until merge).
